# Estimation and tables

- `3_Estimations.do` reproduces the OLS, 2SLS, LIML, and Fuller(1) specifications reported for importing firms in Manufacturing and Wholesale & Retail.
- `4_buildTables.do` converts the long-format result file into the chapter summary and the three appendix tables.

The calendar year 2023 is preserved in all saved outputs. A separate `analysis_year` variable closes the 2022 gap solely for Stata's lag operators.
